STRENGTHS
  • End-to-end online car buying
  • 5-day / 100km trial guarantee
  • Authorized distributor guarantee
  • Cash & installment options
  • Arabic-first platform
  • Financing & insurance integration
WEAKNESSES
  • Limited luxury/premium inventory
  • Fewer total listings than Syarah
  • No vehicle inspection service
  • Limited car review content
  • Narrower model coverage (gaps in 2026)
  • No trade-in service
OPPORTUNITIES
  • EV market growth (Vision 2030)
  • Chinese brand expansion
  • Online buying trend acceleration
  • Expand model coverage rapidly

  • Data monetization from transactions
THREATS
  • Syarah full-stack model threat
